Our Faculty

Spritual

Sadhguru

Sadhguru is an Indian yogi, author, and spiritual teacher who founded the Isha Foundation. He is known for presenting ancient yogic practices and spiritual concepts in a practical and accessible way for modern life.

Maths

R.D. Sharma

R. D. Sharma is a renowned Indian mathematician, educator, and author, best known for his mathematics textbooks used by millions of students across India. He has taught mathematics for many years and is admired for presenting complex concepts in a clear, step-by-step manner.

Physics

Alakh Pandey

Alakh Pandey is the founder of Physics Wallah (PW), an educational company that provides learning resources for students preparing for competitive exams. He is a renowned physics educator known for his unique teaching style and ability to explain difficult concepts in a simple and understandable way.

Chemistry

Anushka Choudhary

Anushka Choudhary is a popular chemistry educator known for her clear and engaging teaching style. She specializes in chemistry and focuses on making difficult concepts easy to understand through simple explanations, practical examples, and effective problem-solving techniques.

English

Aleena Rais

Aleena Rais is a popular English communication coach, YouTuber, and content creator known for helping people improve their spoken English skills. She specializes in English speaking, communication skills, vocabulary, and building confidence while speaking.

Physical Education

Vidyut Jamwal

Vidyut Jammwal is a famous Indian actor, martial artist, and fitness icon known for his exceptional physical strength, flexibility, and discipline. He specializes in martial arts, especially Kalaripayattu, an ancient Indian martial art form, and is recognized for performing many of his own action stunts.

Technology

Love Babbar

Love Babbar is a popular Indian coding educator, software engineer, and YouTuber known for teaching programming and software development skills. He specializes in Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA), C++, web development, and interview preparation for software engineering roles.
